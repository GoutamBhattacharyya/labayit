Delivery Coordinator

Schedule your FREE gift delivery straight to your door at a convenient time for you!

Here’s How it Works!

1

Gifts Are Purchased

Every time a guest purchases a gift for you, the gift will be sent to La’Bayit.

2

Gifts Are Collected

La’Bayit will store all of your gifts so that you don’t have to go to the Post Office all the time!

3

Gifts Are Delivered

A La’Bayit rep will bring all of your gifts to you on the scheduled delivery date for FREE!

Terms & Conditions

1. Couples are eligible for free delivery when:
a. You are a couple that is getting married and has a registry
b. at least 10 items are being delivered that were purchased from the registry.

2. If fewer than 10 items were purchased from the registry, items can be picked up in Jerusalem or delivered for a 79 NIS fee.

3. Gifts can be purchased from a registry until 7 business days before delivery.

4. The recommended delivery date is 12 business days after the wedding so that guests have time to purchase gifts from the registry.

5. Delivery can be scheduled for up to 1 month after the wedding date. Registries will automatically be closed one month after the wedding.

6. Gift cards will be delivered through email.

7. Oversized items and customized items may be delivered separately. Additional shipping fees may apply to the guest at checkout. La’Bayit does not guarantee that all items will be delivered to the couple on the delivery date. Circumstances may require that some items be sent to the couple through a separate delivery service or the Post Office. Couples will not have to pay for shipping for any additional items sent to the couple after the delivery date.
