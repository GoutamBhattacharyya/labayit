GETTING STARTED
What is the minimum number of gifts we can register for?
There is no minimum number of gifts required.
What is group gifting and how do we enable it on our registry?

Group gifting allows your guests to contribute to part of the total cost of a higher-priced item. For example, if you put a $500 coffee machine on your registry, a guest can choose to contribute something like $100 toward that gift. That way, you can receive higher-priced gifts that might not be purchased for you by just one person!

You can enable group gifting on your registry by clicking on the ‘Allow Group Gifting’ button next to an item on your registry. Your guests will then be able to contribute any amount they wish to the gift.

Now go add that coffee machine of your dreams!

Can we add or delete gifts from our registry once it’s made public?
Of course! Even after you make your registry viewable to your guests, you can have fun adding or removing gifts as you please. Please note that once an item is purchased from your registry, you will no longer be able to remove it from your list.
What happens if group gifting doesn’t reach its goal?
Good question! If your gift does not reach its goal, you can choose to contribute the rest of the cost of the item yourself, or receive store credit for other La’Bayit gifts.
What if we want to register for an item but it isn’t on your website?
If you find a product in a store or on a website in Israel that you would like to add to your registry, let us know! We will speak to that store and do our best to add the item to our site.
REGISTRY MANAGEMENT
How do we share our registry with our guests?
With La’Bayit it is super easy to share your registry with your guests. You can send them the link provided to you in the ‘Share Registry’ section.
Can we keep track of who sent us gifts?
Absolutely! Your Dashboard will display a list of each gift that was purchased for you and the awesome guest who purchased it. You will also receive an email notification each time an item is purchased.
Until when can items be purchased from our registry?
Unfortunately, not at this time.
Is there a post-wedding discount?

Yes! Starting the day after your wedding, a 10% discount will be available at checkout for ALL of the items on your registry not yet purchased. The discount will be available until your registry is closed (5 business days before delivery).

Eligibility: To be eligible for the 10% discount couples must have 1) created a registry at least 30 days before the wedding, 2) had at least one item purchased from the registry before the wedding date, 3) have a wedding date specified in their ‘Account’

Terms: The 10% discount is applied towards the item itself, and is not applied to any taxes associated with the item.

RECEIVING GIFTS
When do we receive the items?

Your gifts will all be delivered to you FOR FREE at one specified time, so that you don’t need to deal with the Post Office. The recommended delivery date is 12 business days after your wedding so that your guests have time to purchase gifts for you after your big day. You can customize the delivery date using the ‘Delivery Coordinator’ in your ‘Account’.

Terms & Conditions:

Customers are eligible for free delivery when 1) the customer is a couple that is getting married and has a registry 2) at least 10 items are being delivered that were purchased from the registry. If fewer than 10 items were purchased from the registry, items can be picked up in Jerusalem by the customer or delivered for a 79 NIS fee.

The delivery date must be scheduled within a month after the wedding. Registries will automatically be closed one month after the wedding.

What happens if an item we registered for is out of stock?
Because there is often a gap between the time you register for gifts and when the gifts are actually purchased, an item may be out of stock by the time it is purchased. In such a case, you will receive store credit to purchase a different item from our site. If instead you would like your guest to be reimbursed, please let us know.
Do we have to go to the Post Office to pick up our items?
Nope. One of the greatest perks of La’Bayit is that we hand-deliver all of your gifts at one convenient time FOR FREE, which you customize using our ‘Delivery Coordinator.
Can we return items we no longer want?
Nope! That’s right, shipping is FREE!
